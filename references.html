References & Data

The foundation of this dashboard relies on accurate historical data and open-source research. Below are the primary sources used for data ingestion and domain research.

Data Sources

The raw CSV files processing the dashboard's analytics are sourced from:

  • Kaggle: Formula 1 World Championship (1950 - 2024)

    Maintained by Rohan Rao. This dataset provides the comprehensive history of F1, from which we filter for the modern era (2010-Present).

  • Ergast Developer API

    The original source for the CSV compilations, providing detailed lap times, pit stops, and qualifying data.

Research Materials

Contextual information regarding the 2024 season and the evolution of regulations was cross-referenced with:

  • Wikipedia: 2024 Formula One World Championship

    Used for validating the simplified season calendar and understanding the current constructor lineups and major regulation notes for the "Context" phase of our research.
